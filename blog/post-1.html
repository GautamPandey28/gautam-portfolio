Understanding LTE & 5G Protocol Testing

By Gautam Pandey

Protocol testing ensures that LTE/5G networks follow 3GPP standards. This includes validating procedures such as attach, detach, registration, handover, dedicated bearer setup and more.

Good protocol testing requires:

  • Deep understanding of NAS/RRC/MAC/PHY layers
  • Log analysis skills
  • Use of tools like TTCN-3, Python, RF tools
  • Regression + conformance validation

This is critical for device certification and telecom reliability.
